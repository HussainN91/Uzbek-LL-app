# UNIT 04: Past States & Feelings - Complete Vocab Cards
**Function:** Describe past states and feelings  
**Main Grammar:** was/were + Adjective/Location  
**Uzbek:** O'tmishdagi holat va his-tuyg'ular

## LESSON 1: Past States with was/were

### Dialogue 1: Ali & Sara - Yesterday at School
**Context:** Two friends talking about how they felt yesterday  
**Location:** At school, during break  
**Time:** Today, morning

**Ali:** Salom Sara! Kecha qanday eding?  
Hi Sara! How were you yesterday?

**Sara:** Salom Ali! Men charchagan edim. Sen-chi?  
Hi Ali! I was tired. How about you?

**Ali:** Men ham charchagan edim. Kecha imtihon bor edi.  
I was tired too. Yesterday there was an exam.

**Sara:** Ha, men nervoz edim. Matematika imtihon qiyin edi.  
Yes, I was nervous. The math exam was difficult.

**Ali:** Men ham nervoz edim! Lekin endi xursandman.  
I was nervous too! But now I'm happy.

**Sara:** Nega xursandsan?  
Why are you happy?

**Ali:** Chunki kecha imtihon yaxshi o'tdi!  
Because yesterday the exam went well!

**Sara:** Bu ajoyib! Men ham xursandman endi!  
That's wonderful! I am happy now too!

### VOCAB CARD 1: was

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Sen kecha maktabda emas edingmi?  
You weren't at school yesterday, weren't you?

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men kecha maktabda edim.  
No, I was at school yesterday.

**Hybrid Answer (Uzbek + English):**  
Yo'q, men kecha maktabda **was**. Men u yerda edim.  
No, I **was** at school yesterday. I was there.

**ANCHOR SENTENCE (from dialogue):**  
Men charchagan edim. → I **was** tired.

**Example 2 (P2_UNIT - Different Subject):**  
He **was** at home yesterday.  
U kecha uyda edi.

**Example 3 (P3_RECYCLE - With U01-U03 Vocab):**  
My brother **was** at the library yesterday morning.  
Mening akam kecha ertalab kutubxonada edi.

## LESSON 2: Past Locations with was/were

### Dialogue 2: Malika & Karim - Weekend Activities
**Context:** Two classmates discussing their weekend  
**Location:** School cafeteria  
**Time:** Monday morning

**Malika:** Salom Karim! Hafta oxiri qayerda edingiz?  
Hi Karim! Where were you on the weekend?

**Karim:** Salom Malika! Men shanba kuni muzeyda edim.  
Hi Malika! I was at the museum on Saturday.

**Malika:** Muzey qiziqarli edimi?  
Was the museum interesting?

**Karim:** Ha, juda qiziqarli edi! Sen qayerda edingiz?  
Yes, it was very interesting! Where were you?

**Malika:** Men yakshanba kuni bog'da edim. Ota-onam bilan edim.  
I was at the park on Sunday. I was with my parents.

**Karim:** Ob-havo yaxshi edimi?  
Was the weather good?

**Malika:** Ha, juda yaxshi edi! Biz dam oldik va o'ynadik.  
Yes, it was very good! We rested and played.

**Karim:** Ajoyib! Men ham keyingi yakshanba bog'da bo'laman.  
Wonderful! I will be at the park next Sunday too.

## LESSON 3: Past Questions & Negatives

### Dialogue 3: Laylo & Vali - Asking About Yesterday
**Context:** Two friends meeting at school, discussing yesterday  
**Location:** School entrance  
**Time:** Morning

**Laylo:** Salom Vali! Kecha maktabda edingmi?  
Hi Vali! Were you at school yesterday?

**Vali:** Yo'q, men maktabda emas edim. Men uyda edim.  
No, I wasn't at school. I was at home.

**Laylo:** Nima uchun? Kasal edingmi?  
Why? Were you sick?

**Vali:** Ha, biroz kasal edim. Lekin endi yaxshiman.  
Yes, I was a bit sick. But now I'm better.

**Laylo:** Yaxshi! Men ham kecha uyda edim.  
Good! I was at home yesterday too.

**Vali:** Sen ham kasal edingmi?  
Were you sick too?

**Laylo:** Yo'q, men kasal emas edim. Men faqat charchagan edim.  
No, I wasn't sick. I was just tired.

**Vali:** Tushundim. Endi biz ikkalamiz ham yaxshimiz!  
I understand. Now we are both fine!

## LESSON 4: Extended Past Descriptions

### Dialogue 4: Omar & Dilnoza - Weekend Stories
**Context:** Two students sharing weekend experiences  
**Location:** School yard  
**Time:** Monday, after classes

**Omar:** Salom Dilnoza! Hafta oxiri qayerda edingiz va qanday edingiz?  
Hi Dilnoza! Where were you on the weekend and how were you?

**Dilnoza:** Salom Omar! Men shanba kuni sohilda edim va juda xursand edim!  
Hi Omar! I was at the beach on Saturday and I was very happy!

**Omar:** Ajoyib! Kim bilan edingiz?  
Wonderful! Who were you with?

**Dilnoza:** Men oilam bilan edim. Ob-havo juda yaxshi edi.  
I was with my family. The weather was very good.

**Omar:** Men ham yakshanba kuni tashqarida edim. Men do'stlarim bilan bog'da edim.  
I was outside on Sunday too. I was at the park with my friends.

**Dilnoza:** Nima qildingiz? Futbol o'ynadingizmi?  
What did you do? Did you play football?

**Omar:** Ha, biz futbol o'ynadik va juda hayajonli edik!  
Yes, we played football and we were very excited!

**Dilnoza:** Zo'r! Keyingi yakshanba yana chiqamiz!  
Great! Let's go out again next Sunday!

- All U03 preference verbs (context: "I love the museum")

**New Grammar:**
- was/were (past forms of be)
- wasn't/weren't (past negatives)
- Were you...? / Was he...? (past questions)
- Yes, I was / No, I wasn't (short answers)
