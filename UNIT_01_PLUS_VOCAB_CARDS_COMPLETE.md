## UNIT 01+ - LESSON 01: NEGATIVES & POSSESSION

### Target Vocabulary:
- am not, isn't, aren't (TO BE negatives)
- have, has (possession)
- book, a, an (objects & articles)

### Target Grammar:
- TO BE negatives: I am not / He isn't / They aren't
- HAVE/HAS possession: I have / She has
- Articles: a book, an apple

---

### DIALOGUE 1: Classroom Confusion (Ali & Sara)

**Context:** Two students clarifying identities and possessions

**A (Ali):** Hello, Sara! Are you a teacher?  
**A (Ali):** Salom, Sara! Siz o'qituvchimisiz?

**B (Sara):** No, I am not a teacher. I am a student.  
**B (Sara):** Yo'q, men o'qituvchi emasman. Men talabaman.

**A (Ali):** Is your brother a teacher?  
**A (Ali):** Sizning akangiz o'qituvchimi?

**B (Sara):** No, he isn't a teacher. He is a student too. My father is a teacher.  
**B (Sara):** Yo'q, u o'qituvchi emas. U ham talaba. Mening otam o'qituvchi.

**A (Ali):** Nice! I have a book. Do you have a book?  
**A (Ali):** Zo'r! Menda kitob bor. Sizda kitob bormi?

**B (Sara):** Yes, I have a book. My sister has a book too.  
**B (Sara):** Ha, menda kitob bor. Mening singlimda ham kitob bor.

**A (Ali):** Is your father here?  
**A (Ali):** Sizning otangiz shu yerdami?

**B (Sara):** No, he isn't here. My mother isn't here too. They aren't at school.  
**B (Sara):** Yo'q, u bu yerda emas. Mening onam ham bu yerda emas. Ular maktabda emaslar.

## VOCAB CARDS - A1+ EXTENSIONS

#### VOCAB CARD 1: am not (TO BE negative - first person)

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Siz o'qituvchimisiz?  
(Are you a teacher?)

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men o'qituvchi emasman.  
(No, I am not a teacher.)

**Hybrid Answer:**  
Yo'q, I **am not** a teacher.  
(No, I **am not** a teacher.)

---

**ANCHOR SENTENCE (from dialogue):**  
"No, I **am not** a teacher. I am a student."

**EXAMPLE 2:**  
I **am not** Ali. I am Sara.

**EXAMPLE 3:**  
I **am not** here. I am at home.

**UZBEK TRANSLATION:**  
Emasman (birinchi shaxs inkor)

- I (pronoun)

### Target Grammar:
- THERE IS/ARE: There is a book / There are students
- Location expressions: in school, at home, on the table

---

### DIALOGUE 3: Classroom Description (Sara & Ali)

**Context:** Students describing what's in the classroom

**A (Sara):** Hello, Ali! Is your teacher here?  
**A (Sara):** Salom, Ali! Sizning o'qituvchingiz shu yerdami?

**B (Ali):** Yes, my teacher is here. There is a teacher in the classroom.  
**B (Ali):** Ha, mening o'qituvchim shu yerda. Sinfda o'qituvchi bor.

**A (Sara):** Are there students in the classroom?  
**A (Sara):** Sinfda talabalar bormi?

**B (Ali):** Yes, there are students. There are three students.  
**B (Ali):** Ha, talabalar bor. Uch nafar talaba bor.

**A (Sara):** Is there a book on the table?  
**A (Sara):** Stolda kitob bormi?

**B (Ali):** Yes, there is a book on the table. My brother has a book too.  
**B (Ali):** Ha, stolda kitob bor. Mening akamda ham kitob bor.

**A (Sara):** Are your parents at home?  
**A (Sara):** Ota-onangiz uydami?

**B (Ali):** No, they aren't at home. My father is at work. My mother is at school.  
**B (Ali):** Yo'q, ular uyda emaslar. Mening otam ishda. Mening onam maktabda.

### DIALOGUE 4: Family At Home (Karim & Malika)

**Context:** Two friends discussing their families

**A (Karim):** Hello, Malika! Is your father at home?  
**A (Karim):** Salom, Malika! Sizning otangiz uydami?

**B (Malika):** Yo'q, u uyda emas. U ishda. Lekin mening onam uyda.

**A (Karim):** Is there a book at home?  
**A (Karim):** Uyda kitob bormi?

**B (Malika):** Ha, kitob bor. Mening otamda kitob bor. Uyda uchta kitob bor.

## UNIT 01+ - LESSON 03: WH-QUESTIONS & DEMONSTRATIVES

### Target Vocabulary:

- this, that (demonstratives)

### Target Grammar:
- WH-questions: What is your name? Where is Ali? Who is she?
- Demonstratives: This is my book / That is my father

---

### DIALOGUE 5: Question Practice (Vali & Teacher)

**Context:** Student answering teacher's questions

**A (Teacher):** Hello! What is your name?  
**A (Teacher):** Salom! Ismingiz nima?

**B (Vali):** My name is Vali. Who is she?  
**B (Vali):** Mening ismim Vali. U kim?

**A (Teacher):** U Sara. U talaba. Akangiz qaerda?

**B (Vali):** Mening akam uyda. U maktabda emas. U qachon maktabda?

**A (Teacher):** Otangiz qanday?

**B (Vali):** My father is fine. He is a teacher. This is my father. That is my mother.  
**B (Vali):** Mening otam yaxshi. U o'qituvchi. Bu mening otam. Anikin mening onam.

**A (Teacher):** Nice to meet you!  
**A (Teacher):** Tanishganimdan xursandman!

**B (Vali):** Nice to meet you too!  
**B (Vali):** Men ham tanishganimdan xursandman!

#### VOCAB CARD 23: too (addition marker)

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Faqat siz tanishganimdan xursandmisiz?  
(Only you are pleased to meet?)

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men ham!  
(No, me too!)

**Hybrid Answer:**  
Nice to meet you **too**!  
(Men ham tanishganimdan xursandman!)

---

**ANCHOR SENTENCE (from dialogue):**  
"Nice to meet you **too**!"

**EXAMPLE 2:**  
I am a student **too**.

**EXAMPLE 3:**  
My brother has a book **too**.

**UZBEK TRANSLATION:**  
Ham (qo'shimcha marker)

**RECYCLED FROM UNIT 01:**  
- you, I, my (pronouns/possessives)
- nice, meet, student, brother (greetings/identity/family)
