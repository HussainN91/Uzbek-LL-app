**Function:** Describing rooms and furniture, discussing hobbies and free-time activities, using indefinite pronouns  
**Main Grammar:** There is/are + Indefinite Pronouns, Present Simple with Activities  
**Uzbek:** Xona va mebel tasvirlash, sevimli mashg'ulotlar, gumon olmoshlari

## LESSON 1: Bedroom Furniture & There is/are

### Dialogue 1: Laylo & Akmal - New Bedroom
**Context:** Two siblings discussing the new bedroom  
**Location:** At home, in the new bedroom  
**Time:** Saturday morning

**Laylo:** Salom Akmal! Yangi xonamizni ko'rdingmi?  
Hi Akmal! Did you see our new bedroom?

**Akmal:** Ha, ko'rdim! U juda katta! Nima bor?  
Yes, I saw it! It's very big! What's there?

**Laylo:** Katta karavo'tlar bor. Ikkita karavo't!  
There are big beds. Two beds!

**Akmal:** Ajoyib! Kitob javoni bormi?  
Wonderful! Is there a bookshelf?

**Laylo:** Ha, devor yonida kitob javoni bor. Va stol ham bor!  
Yes, there's a bookshelf by the wall. And there's also a desk!

**Akmal:** Shkaf bormi? Kiyimlarimiz uchun?  
Is there a wardrobe? For our clothes?

**Laylo:** Ha, katta shkaf bor! Va ko'zgu ham bor!  
Yes, there's a big wardrobe! And there's also a mirror!

**Akmal:** Men bu xonani yaxshi ko'raman!  
I love this room!

### VOCAB CARD 1: armchair

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Siz poldagi qattiq stulda o'tirasizmi? Noqulay stulmi?  
You sit on the hard chair on the floor? An uncomfortable chair?

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men qattiq stulda emas. Men yumshoq kresloda o'tiraman.  
No, not on a hard chair. I sit in a soft armchair.

**Hybrid Answer (Uzbek + English):**  
Yo'q, men yumshoq **armchair**da o'tiraman. Kresloda.  
No, I sit in a soft **armchair**. In an armchair.

**ANCHOR SENTENCE (from dialogue):**  
(Extended example)  
Kresloda o'tir. → Sit in the **armchair**.

**Example 2 (P2_UNIT - Different Context):**  
The **armchair** is comfortable.  
Kreslo qulay.

**Example 3 (P3_RECYCLE - With U01-U07 Vocab):**  
I sat in the **armchair** yesterday and watched TV because I was tired.  
Men kecha kresloda o'tirdim va televizor ko'rdim chunki charchagan edim.

## LESSON 3: Indefinite Pronouns

### Dialogue 3: Zilola & Behzod - Looking for Things
**Context:** Two siblings looking for items in their room  
**Location:** At home, in the bedroom  
**Time:** Saturday afternoon

**Zilola:** Salom Behzod! Mening kitobimni ko'rdingmi?  
Hi Behzod! Did you see my book?

**Behzod:** Yo'q, men hech narsani ko'rmadim. Qayerda bo'lishi mumkin?  
No, I didn't see anything. Where could it be?

**Zilola:** Bilmadim. Stolda biror narsa bormi?  
I don't know. Is there something on the desk?

**Behzod:** Yo'q, stolda hech narsa yo'q. Lekin kitob javonida biror narsa bor.  
No, there isn't anything on the desk. But there's something on the bookshelf.

**Zilola:** Xonada kimdir bormi? Kimdir ko'rganmi?  
Is there someone in the room? Did someone see it?

**Behzod:** Yo'q, xonada hech kim yo'q. Hamma tashqarida.  
No, there isn't anyone in the room. Everyone is outside.

**Zilola:** Topildi! Kitob karavot ostida edi!  
Found it! The book was under the bed!

**Behzod:** Yaxshi! Endi hamma narsa joyida!  
Good! Now everything is in its place!
