## LESSON 1: Common Irregular Past Verbs

### Dialogue 1: Aziza & Bobur - Vacation Talk
**Context:** Two classmates discussing summer vacation  
**Location:** School playground  
**Time:** First day of school after summer

**Aziza:** Salom Bobur! Yozda qayerda edingiz?  
Hi Bobur! Where were you in summer?

**Bobur:** Salom Aziza! Men Italiaga bordim! Sen-chi?  
Hi Aziza! I went to Italy! How about you?

**Aziza:** Ajoyib! Men Londonga bordim. Ko'p narsalarni ko'rdim!  
Wonderful! I went to London. I saw many things!

**Bobur:** Nima qilding? Muzeyga bordingmi?  
What did you do? Did you go to a museum?

**Aziza:** Ha, men muzeyga bordim va juda ko'p fotosuratlar oldim.  
Yes, I went to a museum and took many photos.

**Bobur:** Men ham! Men sohilda edim va suzish qildim.  
Me too! I was at the beach and went swimming.

**Aziza:** Men mehmonxonada qoldim. Juda yaxshi edi!  
I stayed at a hotel. It was very good!

**Bobur:** Keyingi yozda yana sayohat qilamiz!  
Next summer we'll travel again!

### VOCAB CARD 1: went (go)

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Sen kecha uyda qoldingmi? Hech qaerga chiqmasdingmi?  
You stayed at home yesterday? You didn't go anywhere?

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men uyda qolmadim. Men bog'ga bordim!  
No, I didn't stay at home. I went to the park!

**Hybrid Answer (Uzbek + English):**  
Yo'q, men kecha bog'ga **went**. Men bordim!  
No, I **went** to the park yesterday. I went!

**ANCHOR SENTENCE (from dialogue):**  
Men Italiaga bordim. → I **went** to Italy.

**Example 2 (P2_UNIT - Different Subject):**  
She **went** to school yesterday.  
U kecha maktabga bordi.

**Example 3 (P3_RECYCLE - With U01-U06 Vocab):**  
My family **went** to the museum last week and we were very happy.  
Mening oilam o'tgan hafta muzeyga bordi va biz juda xursand edik.

## LESSON 3: Past Negatives & Questions with Irregular Verbs

### Dialogue 3: Shoira & Timur - Weekend Plans Gone Wrong
**Context:** Two friends discussing their weekend  
**Location:** School entrance  
**Time:** Monday morning

**Shoira:** Salom Timur! Hafta oxiri muzeyga bordingmi?  
Hi Timur! Did you go to the museum on the weekend?

**Timur:** Yo'q, men muzeyga bormadim. Men uyda qoldim.  
No, I didn't go to the museum. I stayed at home.

**Shoira:** Nima uchun? Kasal edingmi?  
Why? Were you sick?

**Timur:** Ha, biroz kasal edim. Sen muzeyga bordingmi?  
Yes, I was a bit sick. Did you go to the museum?

**Shoira:** Yo'q, men ham bormadim. Men do'stimni ziyorat qildim.  
No, I didn't go either. I visited my friend.

**Timur:** Qiziqarli edimi?  
Was it interesting?

**Shoira:** Ha, juda qiziqarli edi! Biz ko'p gaplashdik va ovqat yedik.  
Yes, it was very interesting! We talked a lot and ate food.

**Timur:** Ajoyib! Keyingi hafta siz bilan muzeyga boraman!  
Wonderful! Next week I'll go to the museum with you!

## LESSON 4: Complex Irregular Past Narratives

### Dialogue 4: Gulnora & Jamshid - Last Summer
**Context:** Two students sharing summer memories  
**Location:** School yard  
**Time:** September, first week

**Gulnora:** Salom Jamshid! O'tgan yozda eng yaxshi kun qaysi edi?  
Hi Jamshid! What was the best day last summer?

**Jamshid:** Salom Gulnora! Men Londonga borganimda! Sen-chi?  
Hi Gulnora! When I went to London! How about you?

**Gulnora:** Men Italiyadagi ko'l oldida yotganimda!  
When I lay by the lake in Italy!

**Jamshid:** Ajoyib! Londonda men ko'p narsalarni ko'rdim va fotosuratlar oldim.  
Wonderful! In London I saw many things and took photos.

**Gulnora:** Men ham! Men muzeyga bordim va sovg'alar sotib oldim.  
Me too! I went to a museum and bought presents.

**Jamshid:** Qiziqarli edi! Men taksiga mindim va shaharni ko'rib chiqdim.  
It was interesting! I rode in a taxi and saw the city.

**Gulnora:** Men velosipedga mindim va tog'ga chiqdim!  
I rode a bike and climbed a mountain!

**Jamshid:** Keyingi yoz yana sayohat qilamiz!  
Next summer we'll travel again!

- All U01 pronouns (I, you, he, she, we, they)
- All U04 was/were (combined with irregular actions)
- All U05 did/didn't (now with irregular base verbs)
- All time expressions from U02-U06

**New Grammar:**
- Irregular past verb forms (went, saw, took, bought, ate, came, had, did, etc.)
- Complete verb change (NOT -ed pattern)
- did/didn't + irregular BASE VERB (NOT past form)
- **CRITICAL RULE:** "Did you go?" NOT "Did you went?"

**Common Irregular Verbs Introduced:**
- go → went, see → saw, take → took, buy → bought
- eat → ate, come → came, have → had, do → did
- ride → rode, lie → lay, make → made, get → got
- give → gave, write → wrote, read → read, leave → left
