## UNIT 03 - LESSON 01: FOOD PREFERENCES

### Target Vocabulary:
- like, love, hate, pizza, burgers, plov, laghman, chocolate, ice cream, breakfast, lunch, dinner

### Target Grammar:
- like/love/hate + noun: I like pizza / She loves music
- Questions: Do you like...? / Does she love...?
- Negatives: I don't like... / He doesn't hate...
- Third person -s: likes, loves, hates

---

### DIALOGUE 1: Food Preferences (Ali & Malika)

**Context:** Two friends discussing their favorite foods

**A (Ali):** Do you like pizza?  
**A (Ali):** Siz pitstsani yaxshi ko'rasizmi?

**B (Malika):** Yes, I love pizza! I eat pizza every weekend. Do you like pizza?  
**B (Malika):** Ha, men pitstsani juda yaxshi ko'raman! Men har hafta oxirida pittsa yeyman. Siz pitstsani yaxshi ko'rasizmi?

**A (Ali):** Yes, I like pizza too. But I love burgers. I have burgers after school.  
**A (Ali):** Ha, men ham pitstsani yaxshi ko'raman. Lekin men burgerlarni juda yaxshi ko'raman. Men maktabdan keyin burger yeyman.

**B (Malika):** I don't like burgers. They are too fatty. My mother likes plov.  
**B (Malika):** Men burgerlarni yaxshi ko'rmayman. Ular juda yog'li. Mening onam palovni yaxshi ko'radi.

**A (Ali):** I love plov! My father cooks plov on weekends. Does your sister like laghman?  
**A (Ali):** Men palovni juda yaxshi ko'raman! Mening otam hafta oxirida palov pishiradi. Sizning opangiz lag'monni yaxshi ko'radimi?

**B (Malika):** Yes, she loves laghman. She eats laghman every day at school. I love chocolate and ice cream.  
**B (Malika):** Ha, u lag'monni juda yaxshi ko'radi. U har kuni maktabda lag'mon yeydi. Men shokolad va muzqaymoqni juda yaxshi ko'raman.

**A (Ali):** Me too! I eat ice cream in the evening. Do you have breakfast every morning?  
**A (Ali):** Men ham! Men kechqurun muzqaymoq yeyman. Siz har ertalab nonushta qilasizmi?

**B (Malika):** Yes, I have breakfast at home. I love breakfast. My brother doesn't like breakfast.  
**B (Malika):** Ha, men uyda nonushta qilaman. Men nonushtani juda yaxshi ko'raman. Mening akam nonushtani yaxshi ko'rmaydi.

## UNIT 03 - LESSON 03: ASKING PREFERENCES

- Short answers: Yes, I do / No, I don't

### DIALOGUE 3: Finding Common Interests (Laylo & Vali)

**Context:** Two classmates getting to know each other

**A (Laylo):** What do you like?  
**A (Laylo):** Siz nimani yaxshi ko'rasiz?

**B (Vali):** I like sports. I play football and do athletics. What do you like?  
**B (Vali):** Men sportni yaxshi ko'raman. Men futbol o'ynayman va yengil atletika bilan shug'ullanaman. Siz nimani yaxshi ko'rasiz?

**A (Laylo):** I love music and movies. Do you like music?  
**A (Laylo):** Men musiqa va kinolarni juda yaxshi ko'raman. Siz musiqani yaxshi ko'rasizmi?

**B (Vali):** Yes, I do. I play in a band. What music do you like?  
**B (Vali):** Ha, yaxshi ko'raman. Men guruhda o'ynayman. Siz qanday musiqani yaxshi ko'rasiz?

**A (Laylo):** I like all music! My sister loves music too. She wants to become a singer.  
**A (Laylo):** Men barcha musiqani yaxshi ko'raman! Mening opam ham musiqani juda yaxshi ko'radi. U qo'shiqchi bo'lishni xohlaydi.

**B (Vali):** That's great! What food does she like?  
**B (Vali):** Bu ajoyib! U qanday ovqatni yaxshi ko'radi?

**A (Laylo):** She loves chocolate and ice cream. She eats ice cream every day. Do you like chocolate?  
**A (Laylo):** U shokolad va muzqaymoqni juda yaxshi ko'radi. U har kuni muzqaymoq yeydi. Siz shokoladni yaxshi ko'rasizmi?

**B (Vali):** Yes, I do! I eat chocolate after dinner. I also love pizza and plov.  
**B (Vali):** Ha, yaxshi ko'raman! Men kechki ovqatdan keyin shokolad yeyman. Men pittsa va palovni ham juda yaxshi ko'raman.

### DIALOGUE 4: Talking About People (Omar & Malika)

**Context:** Two friends discussing their teachers and classmates

**A (Omar):** Do you like your teacher?  
**A (Omar):** Siz o'qituvchingizni yaxshi ko'rasizmi?

**B (Malika):** Yes, I do! She is very kind and helpful. I like kind teachers.  
**B (Malika):** Ha, yaxshi ko'raman! U juda mehribon va foydali. Men mehribon o'qituvchilarni yaxshi ko'raman.

**A (Omar):** Me too! My teacher is friendly. I love friendly people.  
**A (Omar):** Men ham! Mening o'qituvchim do'stona. Men do'stona odamlarni juda yaxshi ko'raman.

**B (Malika):** Does your brother like his teacher?  
**B (Malika):** Sizning akangiz o'z o'qituvchisini yaxshi ko'radimi?

**A (Omar):** Yes, he does. His teacher is funny. He likes funny teachers.  
**A (Omar):** Ha, yaxshi ko'radi. Uning o'qituvchisi kulgili. U kulgili o'qituvchilarni yaxshi ko'radi.

**B (Malika):** I like smart classmates. My classmates are very smart.  
**B (Malika):** Men aqlli sinfdoshlarimni yaxshi ko'raman. Mening sinfdoshlarim juda aqlli.

**A (Omar):** My sister likes quiet places. She studies at the library every day.  
**A (Omar):** Mening opam jim joylarni yaxshi ko'radi. U har kuni kutubxonada o'qiydi.

**B (Malika):** I like the library too. It is very quiet. I study there on weekends.  
**B (Malika):** Men ham kutubxonani yaxshi ko'raman. U juda jim. Men u yerda hafta oxirida o'qiyman.

#### VOCAB CARD 24: friendly

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Siz qattiq odamlarni yaxshi ko'rasizmi?  
(Do you like strict people?)

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men do'stona odamlarni yaxshi ko'raman.  
(No, I like friendly people.)

**Hybrid Answer:**  
Yo'q, men **friendly** odamlarni yaxshi ko'raman.  
(No, I like **friendly** people.)

---
