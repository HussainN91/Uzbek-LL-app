## UNIT 01 - LESSON 01: NAMES & IDENTITY

### Target Vocabulary:
- name, I, you, he, she, teacher, student, my, his, her

### Target Grammar:
- TO BE affirmative: I am / You are / He is / She is / They are
- Yes/No questions: Are you...? Is he...?
- Possessives: My name, His name, Her name

---

### DIALOGUE 1: Meeting Someone New (Ali & Sara)

**Context:** Two students meeting for the first time at school

**A (Ali):** Hello! My name is Ali.  
**A (Ali):** Salom! Mening ismim Ali.

**B (Sara):** Hello, Ali! I am Sara.  
**B (Sara):** Salom, Ali! Men Sara.

**A (Ali):** Nice to meet you, Sara. Are you a teacher?  
**A (Ali):** Seni tanishib o'tganimdan xursandman, Sara. Siz o'qituvchimisiz?

**B (Sara):** No, I am a student. Are you a student too?  
**B (Sara):** Yo'q, men talabaman. Siz ham talabamissiz?

**A (Ali):** Yes, I am a student. What is his name?  
**A (Ali):** Ha, men talabaman. Uning ismi nima?

**B (Sara):** His name is Karim. He is a teacher. This is my mother. Her name is Malika.  
**B (Sara):** Uning ismi Karim. U o'qituvchi. Bu mening onam. Uning ismi Malika.

**A (Ali):** Nice to meet you, Malika.  
**A (Ali):** Seni tanishib o'tganimdan xursandman, Malika.

### DIALOGUE 2: Family Introduction (Vali & Teacher)

**Context:** Student introducing his family to a teacher

**A (Teacher):** Hello! What is your name?  
**A (Teacher):** Salom! Ismingiz nima?

**B (Vali):** I am Vali. My father is Omar. My mother is Laylo.  
**B (Vali):** Men Vali. Mening otam Omar. Mening onam Laylo.

**A (Teacher):** Nice to meet you, Vali! Is your brother here?  
**A (Teacher):** Seni tanishib o'tganimdan xursandman, Vali! Sizning akangiz shu yerda?

**B (Vali):** Yes, he is here. His name is Karim. We are students.  
**B (Vali):** Ha, u shu yerda. Uning ismi Karim. Biz talabalarmiz.

**A (Teacher):** Nice to meet you all!  
**A (Teacher):** Hammaningizni tanishib o'tganimdan xursandman!

(Your name is Ali?)

(No, my name is Sara.)
