## SECTION A: PRESENT SIMPLE FOUNDATION (Original Content)

### DIALOGUE 1: Morning Routine (8 turns)
A: Good morning! Do you wake up early?
B: Yes, I wake up at 6 AM every day.
A: What do you do first?
B: I brush my teeth and take a shower.
A: I don't like mornings. Do you exercise?
B: Yes, I run every morning. It's good for you.
A: That sounds nice. Do you eat breakfast?
B: Yes, I eat a big breakfast at 7 AM.

### VOCAB CARD 1: wake (up)
**Uzbek Context Question:** Siz qachon uyg'onasiz?
**Mirror/Hybrid Answer:** I wake up at 6 AM. She wakes up early.
**Anchor Sentence:** He wakes up at 7 o'clock.
**Example 2:** We wake up early on weekdays.
**Example 3:** Do you wake up late on weekends?
**Uzbek Translation:** Men 6 da uyg'onaman. U erta uyg'onadi.
