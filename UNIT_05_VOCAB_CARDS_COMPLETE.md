## LESSON 1: Regular Past Actions (-ED)

### Dialogue 1: Kamola & Rashid - Weekend Activities
**Context:** Two friends discussing what they did last weekend  
**Location:** School cafeteria  
**Time:** Monday, lunchtime

**Kamola:** Salom Rashid! Hafta oxiri nima qilding?  
Hi Rashid! What did you do on the weekend?

**Rashid:** Salom Kamola! Men shanba kuni futbol o'ynadim. Sen-chi?  
Hi Kamola! I played football on Saturday. How about you?

**Kamola:** Men uyda dam oldim va televizor ko'rdim.  
I rested at home and watched TV.

**Rashid:** Yaxshi! Yakshanba kuni nima qilding?  
Good! What did you do on Sunday?

**Kamola:** Men do'stlarimni ziyorat qildim. Biz muzeyga bordik.  
I visited my friends. We went to the museum.

**Rashid:** Ajoyib! Muzey qiziqarli edimi?  
Wonderful! Was the museum interesting?

**Kamola:** Ha, juda qiziqarli edi! Biz ko'p narsalarni ko'rdik.  
Yes, it was very interesting! We saw many things.

**Rashid:** Men ham keyingi hafta muzeygga boraman!  
I will go to the museum next week too!

---

### VOCAB CARD 1: played

**Uzbek Context Question:**  
Sen kecha dam oldingmi? Uyda yotdingmi?  
You rested yesterday? You lay at home?

**Uzbek Mirror Answer:**  
Yo'q, men dam olmadim. Men futbol o'ynadim!  
No, I didn't rest. I played football!

**Hybrid Answer (Uzbek + English):**  
Yo'q, men kecha futbol **played**. Men o'ynadim!  
No, I **played** football yesterday. I played!

**ANCHOR SENTENCE (from dialogue):**  
Men shanba kuni futbol o'ynadim. → I **played** football on Saturday.

**Example 2 (P2_UNIT - Different Subject):**  
She **played** volleyball yesterday evening.  
U kecha kechqurun voleybol o'ynadi.

**Example 3 (P3_RECYCLE - With U01-U03 Vocab):**  
My brother **played** computer games after school because he loves games.  
Mening akam maktabdan keyin kompyuter o'yinlari o'ynadi chunki u o'yinlarni yaxshi ko'radi.

## LESSON 2: Past Questions & Negatives (did/didn't)

### Dialogue 2: Anvar & Zarina - Talking About Yesterday
**Context:** Two classmates discussing yesterday's activities  
**Location:** School hallway  
**Time:** Morning, before class

**Anvar:** Salom Zarina! Kecha maktabga keldingmi?  
Hi Zarina! Did you come to school yesterday?

**Zarina:** Yo'q, men maktabga kelmadim. Men uyda edim.  
No, I didn't come to school. I was at home.

**Anvar:** Nima uchun? Kasal edingmi?  
Why? Were you sick?

**Zarina:** Yo'q, men kasal emas edim. Faqat charchagan edim.  
No, I wasn't sick. I was just tired.

**Anvar:** Tushundim. Uyda nima qilding?  
I understand. What did you do at home?

**Zarina:** Men kitob o'qidim va musiqa tingladim. Sen nima qilding?  
I read a book and listened to music. What did you do?

**Anvar:** Men maktabga bordim va matematika o'qidim.  
I went to school and studied math.

**Zarina:** Yaxshi! Endi men ham maktabdaman!  
Good! Now I'm at school too!

## LESSON 3: Extended Past Narratives

### Dialogue 3: Sardor & Nigora - Last Week Story
**Context:** Two friends sharing stories about last week  
**Location:** School library  
**Time:** After classes

**Sardor:** Salom Nigora! O'tgan hafta nima qilding?  
Hi Nigora! What did you do last week?

**Nigora:** Salom Sardor! Men juda ko'p narsalar qildim!  
Hi Sardor! I did many things!

**Sardor:** Menga aytib ber! Men eshitmoqchiman.  
Tell me! I want to hear.

**Nigora:** Dushanba kuni maktabda o'qidim. Seshanba kuni do'stlarimni ziyorat qildim.  
On Monday I studied at school. On Tuesday I visited my friends.

**Sardor:** Ajoyib! Davom et!  
Wonderful! Continue!

**Nigora:** Chorshanba kuni onamga uyda yordam berdim. Payshanba kuni kutubxonada kitob o'qidim.  
On Wednesday I helped my mother at home. On Thursday I read a book at the library.

**Sardor:** Juma kuni-chi?  
What about Friday?

**Nigora:** Juma kuni futbol o'ynadim va shanba kuni muzeyga bordim!  
On Friday I played football and on Saturday I went to the museum!

## LESSON 4: Complex Past Activities

### Dialogue 4: Feruza & Jasur - Busy Week
**Context:** Two students discussing their busy week  
**Location:** School cafeteria  
**Time:** Friday afternoon

**Feruza:** Salom Jasur! Bu hafta juda band edim!  
Hi Jasur! This week I was very busy!

**Jasur:** Salom Feruza! Men ham! Nima qilding?  
Hi Feruza! Me too! What did you do?

**Feruza:** Men har kuni maktabda o'qidim. Keyin uyda yordam berdim.  
I studied at school every day. Then I helped at home.

**Jasur:** Men ham! Men onamga ovqat pishirishda yordam berdim.  
Me too! I helped my mother cook food.

**Feruza:** Ajoyib! Sen do'stlaring bilan vaqt o'tkazdingmi?  
Wonderful! Did you spend time with friends?

**Jasur:** Ha, chorshanba kuni do'stlarimni ziyorat qildim. Biz futbol o'ynadik.  
Yes, on Wednesday I visited my friends. We played football.

**Feruza:** Men ham payshanba kuni do'stlarim bilan muzeyga bordim!  
I also went to the museum with friends on Thursday!

**Jasur:** Endi dam olish vaqti! Hafta oxiri dam olamiz!  
Now it's time to rest! We'll rest on the weekend!

## Summary

**Total Vocab Cards: 30**
- Lesson 1: Regular Past Actions -ed (8 cards)
- Lesson 2: Past Questions & Negatives did/didn't (8 cards)
- Lesson 3: Extended Past Narratives (8 cards)
- Lesson 4: Complex Past Activities (6 cards)

**Total Dialogues: 4**
- Each dialogue: 6-8 turns
- All turns: English + Uzbek translation
- Natural conversation flow
- Grammar: Past Simple (-ed), did/didn't in (+), (-), (?) forms
- Recycling: 60-70% from U01-U04

**Recycled Grammar:**
- All U01 pronouns (I, you, he, she, we, they)
- All U02 present simple verbs → now past -ed forms
- All U04 was/were (combined with actions)
- All time expressions from U02-U04

**New Grammar:**
- Regular past -ed forms (played, worked, watched, etc.)
- did/didn't (past auxiliary for questions/negatives)
- Did you...? (past questions with base verb)
- Yes, I did / No, I didn't (short answers)
- **CRITICAL RULE:** After did/didn't, use BASE VERB (not -ed)
